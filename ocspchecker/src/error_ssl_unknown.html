Unknown TLS/SSL Certificate Revocation State

The revocation state of at least one TLS/SSL Certificate cannot be determined and due to the strict policy set in the plugin's option page this error page is shown.



This could have one or more of the following reasons:

  • The servers of the OCSP Checker extension cannot be reached to determine the TLS/SSL Certificates state.
  • One of the OCSP Responders defined in the TLS/SSL Certificate is offline.
  • One of the OCSP Responders defined in the TLS/SSL Certificate responded with an unknown revocation state.
net::ERR_CERT_STATE_UNKNOWN
